What is Emotional Intelligence?

It is the ability to understand, process, use and manage either our own or othersâ€™ emotions in a positive way. It is important to guide our behaviour and adapt to different environments or circumstances which impact our mental health, job/ study performance and all social relationships.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is the ability to resolve problems using logic or our previous knowledge, it is the capacity of learning, meanwhile EQ is related to interpersonal abilities showing our capacity to perform with other people in multiple different contexts.

Why is it important?

It is important because it can increase or improve our performance in any field based on how we interpret and behave in a specific context. In addition, it can make our relationships more meaningful.
